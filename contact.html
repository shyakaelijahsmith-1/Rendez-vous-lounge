Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Rendez Vous Lounge

Do I need a reservation?

While we do accept walk-ins when space is available, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner service and weekends. You can make a reservation online or by calling us directly.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we accommodate various dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergies. Please inform us of any dietary requirements when making your reservation or speak with your server.

Do you offer private dining?

Yes, we have a private dining room that can accommodate up to 30 guests. We also offer semi-private dining options for smaller groups. Please contact our events team for more information and availability.

What is your dress code?

We recommend smart casual attire. While we don't have a strict dress code, we ask guests to avoid athletic wear, beachwear, and overly casual clothing. Jackets are preferred but not required.

Do you have parking available?

We offer valet parking service for $25 per vehicle. There are also several public parking garages within walking distance. Street parking is available but can be limited during peak hours.
