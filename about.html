Our Story

Rendez Vous Lounge was established as Kigali's premier destination for exceptional dining and entertainment. Our vision is to create a vibrant space where premium cuisine meets lively nightlife, offering guests an unforgettable experience in the heart of Rwanda's capital.

What started as a dream to bring world-class entertainment and dining to Kigali has grown into one of the city's most celebrated venues, earning a loyal following of patrons who appreciate quality, atmosphere, and exceptional service.

Today, Rendez Vous Lounge continues to elevate the dining and entertainment scene, creating experiences that celebrate both local Rwandan culture and international sophistication.
